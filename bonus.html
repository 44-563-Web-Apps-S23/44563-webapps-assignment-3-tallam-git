Saffron Tiger

My super-hero is Saffron Tiger, he usually have a red cape and mask to his eyes. He have a muscular body with strong forelimbs, a large head and a tail that is about half the length of its body. He have a mission to serve, which is to protect the forest lands from man-kind and saves the fellow animals from dangers.

  1. He have an infallible memory, as their short-term memory lasts about thirty times longer than normal ones
  2. He have super strength
  3. He flies very fast.
