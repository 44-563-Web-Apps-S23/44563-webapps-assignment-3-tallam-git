Favorite Book: The Girl in Room 105

The girl in room 105 is a thriller novel written by Chetan Bhagat. This book keeps you hooked util the End. I was so intrigued to find out who killed Zara (the girl who is murdered in the novel) that I couldn't stop reading. Though my Speculative guess about the killer was absolutely right, (by the time all characters were introduced) but I was wrong about the motive and the reason why she was killed. There are many such twists and tales in the novel, which is one of the primary to love the book.
